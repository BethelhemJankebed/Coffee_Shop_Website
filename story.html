The Abyssinia Legacy

Abyssinia Coffee was founded on a simple belief: that coffee is more than just a drink—it's a shared heritage. Our journey began in the lush highlands of Ethiopia, the birthplace of Arabica coffee, where we hand-picked our first harvest.

Today, we continue that tradition of excellence, sourcing directly from local farmers to ensure every bean tells a story of quality, sustainability, and community.
